DWCRA (Development of Women and Children in Rural Areas) is dedicated to fostering self-reliance and economic independence through community-led self-help groups.

Discover Our Impact Get Involved

Self-Help Groups

We organize women into small groups of 10-15 to promote collective savings and income-generating activities.

Skill Development

Training programs tailored to local needs, empowering women with tools to succeed in their own communities.

Financial Independence

Providing access to credit and resources that were previously out of reach for rural women.

Join the Mahila Scheme

The Mahila Scheme is a flagship initiative under DWCRA aimed at strengthening the socio-economic status of rural women through dedicated support systems and collective action.
