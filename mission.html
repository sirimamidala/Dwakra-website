Our Mission

"To provide support and resources for women to achieve economic independence."

Primary Objectives

The main objective of DWCRA is to improve the socio-economic, health, and educational status of rural women by providing financial assistance and creating employment opportunities.

We aim for women to become self-reliant and to raise their standard of living, specifically targeting families living below the poverty line.

Strategic Goals

  • ✅ Examine the progress of the scheme
  • ✅ Assess success in achieving stipulated objectives
  • ✅ Identify and resolve constraints in unit performance

The overall progress of implementation of the programme is continuously assessed with the help of data from the District Rural Development Authority (DRDA) and various Block Panchayat offices.
